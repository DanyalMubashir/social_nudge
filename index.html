Privacy Policy for SocialNudge

Effective date: April 20, 2026

Contact Nudge helps users stay in touch with people they care about by working with the Contacts framework on iPhone.

Information We Access

Contact Nudge may access selected contact information stored on your device so that you can choose people to add to your circle and receive reminders to check in with them.

How We Use Information

All contact information used by Contact Nudge is processed only on your device to power reminders, contact selection, and core app functionality.

Data Collection

Contact Nudge does not collect, store, transmit, or sell your personal data on external servers. Your data remains on your device.

Third Parties

Contact Nudge does not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking tools in v1.

Notifications

If you allow notifications, Contact Nudge uses local notifications on your device to remind you when it may be time to reach out to someone in your circle.

Your Choices

You can revoke access to Contacts or Notifications at any time in your iPhone Settings.
